How Apogee works

Apogee is a self-custody wallet for the Liquid Network — a Bitcoin sidechain with fast, confidential transactions. You hold your own keys; Resolvr never sees them.

  1. 1

    Set up your wallet

    Create a new wallet, restore from a recovery phrase, or pair a Blockstream Jade hardware wallet.

  2. 2

    Receive

    Generate a fresh confidential Liquid address for each deposit — amounts and assets stay private on-chain.

  3. 3

    Send

    Enter an address and amount, review the fee, and approve. Apogee builds, signs, and broadcasts the transaction.

  4. 4

    Connect to web apps

    Compatible sites request a connection; you approve each connection and every send. Review or revoke connected apps in Settings.

  5. 5

    Stay in control

    Your recovery phrase, keys, and password never leave this device. Lock Apogee anytime, or let it auto-lock after inactivity.
